## Token Management
Crtitical from 2 aspectes:  
1. Agent level/context windows.  Managing these is single most important delivery aspect.  
2. Reduction in token usage:  I believe managing long term memory is not the job of the LLM.  Pulling more of that away form LLM as possible is a goal.  LLM agents need the right context, at the right time.  Developing the system to manage that outside of the LLM is my goal.
